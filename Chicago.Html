Chicago Pizza

  • Deep-dish pizza is baked in a round, steel pan that is more similar to a cake or pie pan than a typical pizza pan.
  • Most used toppings on deep dish include: Pepperoni, Sausage, Bacon, Ham and various other meats
  • First Deep Dish Pizza was made by none other than Pizzeria Uno in the early 1950's.
  • The top layer of sauce remains uncooked and is the last layer added.
  • ALthough the pizza is thick, the actual crust is thin to medium in thickness.
